Pause

Vish, come back to yourself.

You opened Facebook automatically. That is not failure. It is a cue to stop, breathe, and choose the next minute on purpose.

This is part of the real work of your life: learning how to regulate attention, tolerate discomfort, and return to what matters.
Close the loop Shut this tab and go back to the task you meant to do. Take a real break Reset your nervous system instead of numbing out. Name the urge Write one sentence: “Right now I am avoiding…”
Stay here for one breath.
You do not need to be perfect. You only need to interrupt the reflex once, right now.
